Artificial Intelligence

The term AI, coined in the 1950s, refers to the simulation of human intelligence by machines. It includes machine learning and deep learning.

By Ishan, XI-A

2022: AI in the Mainstream

AI gained popularity due to ChatGPT and Generative Pre-Training Transformers. But ChatGPT is just a small part of AI’s broader usage.

Key Takeaways

  • AI simulates human intelligence in machines.
  • Used in learning, reasoning, and perception.
  • AI is used in healthcare, finance, and more.
  • Weak AI = simple tasks; Strong AI = human-like abilities.

Goals of AI

  • Reasoning and problem-solving
  • Handling uncertainty and probability
  • Fast decision-making using heuristics

AI Tools

  • Search & Optimization
  • Planning Algorithms
  • Robotics and Object Handling

Deep Learning

Deep learning uses multiple layers to extract features from data. It’s used in speech recognition, image analysis, and more.
